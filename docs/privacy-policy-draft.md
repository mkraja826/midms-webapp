## 2. Purpose of the app

The app is used by dental clinics to manage clinic operations such as patient check-ins, dental visit records, prescriptions, X-rays, payments, appointments, follow-ups, and staff access.

The app is not a medical diagnosis tool and does not provide emergency medical services.

## 3. Information collected

The app may collect the following information when entered by clinic staff:

- Patient name
- Patient phone number
- Age or basic profile details
- Medical history notes entered by the clinic
- Dental visit details
- Chief complaint category
- Treatment and billing details
- Prescription images
- X-ray images
- Before/after treatment photos
- Appointment and follow-up details
- Staff login and role information

## 4. How information is used

Information is used only for clinic management purposes, including:

- Identifying patients
- Maintaining patient visit history
- Managing doctor queues
- Uploading prescriptions and X-rays
- Tracking payments and pending balances
- Booking follow-up appointments
- Sending WhatsApp reminders when initiated by clinic staff
- Managing staff roles and access
- Showing owner/admin revenue and workflow summaries

## 5. Data sharing

The app does not sell patient data.

Patient data may be visible to authorized clinic staff based on their role. Data may be stored using trusted cloud services required for app operation.

Data may be disclosed only when required by law or with clinic authorization.

## 6. Data storage and security

The app uses authentication and role-based access to reduce unauthorized access.

Clinic staff should keep login credentials private and should not share accounts.

No software system can guarantee absolute security, but reasonable safeguards should be used to protect clinic and patient data.

## 7. Data retention

Clinic data may be retained as long as the clinic uses the app or as required for clinic record keeping.

The clinic owner may request export or deletion support where technically and legally possible.

## 8. User rights

Patients should contact the clinic directly for access, correction, or deletion requests related to their clinic records.

Clinic staff should contact the app owner/admin for account or access issues.

## 9. Children's data

The app may contain patient records for children if entered by a dental clinic. Such data is handled as clinic record data and should be entered only by authorized clinic staff with appropriate consent where required.
