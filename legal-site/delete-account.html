How to request deletion

To request deletion of your MiDMS account or clinic data, email support@micirql.com with the following details:

  • Clinic name
  • Registered email address
  • Phone number
  • Deletion scope: account only, clinic data, patient records, uploaded files or all data
  • Proof that you are authorized to request deletion for the clinic

Processing

We will review eligible deletion requests within 30 days, unless retention is required for legal, security or legitimate operational reasons.
