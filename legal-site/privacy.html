Privacy Policy

MiDMS - Dental Management System

Last updated: July 2026

1. App name and developer

MiDMS, also known as Dental Management System, is a dental clinic management application developed for dental clinic workflow management.

2. Data we collect

MiDMS may collect and store data entered by clinic owners or authorized clinic staff, including:

  • Clinic account details and staff account details such as name, email, role and login information.
  • Patient details such as name, age, phone number and dental/medical history.
  • Dental records such as visits, treatments, prescriptions, X-rays, patient photos and follow-up details.
  • Payment records such as OP fees, treatment/medication fees and pending payment status.
  • Technical information required to operate, secure and maintain the app.

3. How we use data

We use this data to provide clinic management features, maintain patient records, support appointments and follow-ups, manage staff access, upload clinical files, track payments and secure the service.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell clinic or patient data. Data may be processed by trusted service providers only for app hosting, authentication, database, storage, security and support operations.

5. Data security

MiDMS uses encrypted connections for data transmission. Clinics are responsible for allowing access only to authorized staff and for following applicable healthcare, privacy and patient consent requirements.

6. Data deletion

Authorized clinic owners may request account or clinic data deletion by visiting Account & Data Deletion or by contacting support@micirql.com.
