# DMS Privacy Policy

DMS is a clinic workflow app for dental clinics. Clinic staff use it to manage patients, appointments, visits, prescriptions, X-rays, files, reminders, and payment records.

## Data Collected

- Clinic account and staff profile details
- Patient name, phone, age, gender, address, and emergency contact
- Medical history notes, visit notes, treatment notes, and follow-up details
- Prescription photos, X-ray photos, before/after photos, reports, and uploaded patient files
- Appointment, reminder, invoice, payment, and pending balance records

## Data Use

Data is used only for clinic workflow, patient care coordination, billing, reminders, and clinic administration. DMS does not sell patient or clinic data.

## Data Storage

Clinic and patient data is stored in Supabase. Access is restricted by clinic membership and role-based rules.

## Deletion Requests

Patients or clinic staff can request correction or deletion of data by contacting the clinic owner/admin. The clinic owner is responsible for handling data deletion requests according to local clinical record rules.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the clinic owner/admin using the contact details provided by the clinic.
